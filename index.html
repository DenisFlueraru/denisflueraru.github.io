2024 > Recycle your Technology, BRD Open Call

As an artist chosen by the RYT team I made an interactive installation called i:105, constructed from recycled electronic waste provided by Eco Positive Romania. I also conducted workshops in four Romanian cities (Reșița, Arad, Brașov, and Bucharest), where participants learned to create their own EMF microphones in order to capture and creatively interpret electromagnetic fields, blending environmental awareness with artistic exploration.
