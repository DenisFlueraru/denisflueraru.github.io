Contact Mics & Preamps

Participants built DIY microphones together with preamps and explored recording techniques, later composing textures and rhythms in Ableton Live.

Year: 2025 – Bucharest, CAV Gallery

Recycle Your Technology

A hands-on workshop where participants soldered and assembled electromagnetic microphones, then used them to capture electromagnetic rhythms in the gallery space.

This workshop later evolved into a series across Romania, where participants from different cities learned how to build microphones and explore local soundscapes.

Year: 2024 – Bucharest, Arad, Resita, Brasov

Contact Microphones & Composition

We explored the urban environment with microphones, creating a sonic map of the city and later transformed the sounds into a collaborative composition.

Year: 2023 – NOVA Festival, Bucharest

Sonic Explorations @ Pixels from a Past Future

The pilot musique concrète workshop, where participants learned the foundation elements of experimental music and got to build their first contact microphone and later got familiar with Ableton Live by making a collaborative composition.

Year: 2023 – National Museum of Contemporary Art (MNAC), Bucharest
